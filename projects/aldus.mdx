---
title: Aldus - Contributing Chain Data
---

Users, developers, and projects can add and contribute labelled chain data to Celatone by visiting our [Aldus](https://github.com/alleslabs/aldus) repository and opening a pull request. Currently, we are accepting contributions for the types of labels

- Addresses
- Contracts
- Assets
- Codes (CosmWasm Chains Only)
- Contracts (CosmWasm Chains Only)
- Pools (Osmosis Only)
- Modules (Initia Only)

General instructions for contributing can be found in the repository's [README](https://github.com/alleslabs/aldus/blob/main/README.md).
