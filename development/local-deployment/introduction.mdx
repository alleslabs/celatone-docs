In addition to the official instances of Celatone hosted and maintained by the Alles Labs team, there may be instances where developers and users might want to spin up and host their own local version of the webapp.

For examples, chain developers might want to spin up a local explorer to test the functionalities of a pre-release chain version, or smart contract developers to more easily develop smart contracts using a local chain instance.

The following guide will walk you through the steps to spin up a local instance of Celatone for such cases.
